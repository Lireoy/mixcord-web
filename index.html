Features

Mixcord makes it easy to keep up with your favorite Mixer streamers.

Get a message in your Discord server when your favorite streamers go live. You can change the notification's appearance to what fits your server best.

Quickly add, manage, and remove notifications set up for your server with simple commands.

Curious about a streamer? Check their current status without opening the browser. If they are live, you can check what game they are playing, how many viewers they have, the language of the stream and whether their content is suitable for your taste.

Get lazy with Mixcord

You won't have to check Mixer again and again to see if someone is streaming or not, get comfortable and click just once!
